Land Breeze and Sea Breeze

What is sea breeze?

Start Activities

A sea breeze is a cool wind that blows from the ocean to the land during the day. It occurs because land heats up faster than water, creating a low-pressure area over land. Cooler, high-pressure air from the ocean moves in to replace the rising warm air, bringing a refreshing breeze to coastal areas.

What is land breeze?

A land breeze is a gentle wind that moves from the land to the sea at night. It happens because land cools down faster than water, creating a high-pressure area over land. Warm air over the ocean rises, and cooler air from the land moves in to take its place, forming a land breeze.
