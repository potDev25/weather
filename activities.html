ACTIVITY 1

Objective: Explain what happens when air is heated.

Materials:

Two paper bags, Candle, Long straight stick, Match, Masking tape, Chair


Procedure

1. Attach a paper bag to each end of
the stick. The open end of each bag should be facing down.

2. Balance the stick with the paper bags on the chair.

3. Make a prediction: what do you think will happen if you place
a lighted candle under the open end of
one of the bags?

4. Now, light the candle and place it below one of the bags.
Caution: Do not place the candle too close to the paper bag. It may catch fire. Be ready with
a pail of water or wet rag just in case.

Q1. Was your prediction accurate? Describe what happened.

Q2. Can you explain why?

ACTIVITY 2

Objective: Explain what happens to the air in the surrounding as warm air rises.

Materials:

Box, Candle, Smoke source ( e.g. . Mosquito coil ),
Lighter, Clear plastic, Cardboard, Scissors

Procedures

Step 1: Make a box and cut the front part of it

Step 2: Place a hole on the top and side of the box

Step 3: Make a window and cover it with plastic so you can see the inside clearly

Step 4: Open the top of the box and gently put inside
the candle. Make sure that the candle is below the hole of the top

Step 5: Place a cardboard in the hole of the top and tape it .

Step 6: When everything is done, light the candle through the hole in the side and place
the mosquito coil outside the hole for smoke source.

Guide questions:

1. What do you think will happen if you place the smoke source near the hole?

2. What happened?

3. Can you explain why it happened?
